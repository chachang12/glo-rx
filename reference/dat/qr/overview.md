# DAT Quantitative Reasoning — Section Overview

Stub for Phase 0 corpus-loader verification. Replace with real reference

## Topic scope (40 questions, 45-minute section)

- **Algebra** — equations, inequalities, exponents, logarithms, sequences
  and series.
- **Numerical Calculations** — arithmetic, fractions, decimals, percents,
  ratios.
- **Conversions** — between units, metric/imperial, time, percentage.
- **Probability & Statistics** — counting, conditional probability, mean /
  median / mode, standard deviation.
- **Geometry** — area, perimeter, volume, similar triangles, the Pythagorean
  theorem.
- **Trigonometry** — basic ratios, the unit circle, identities.
- **Applied Mathematics (Word Problems)** — translating prose to equations
  (rates, work, mixture, age problems).
- **Data Analysis** — reading tables, charts, and graphs.

## Question pattern notes

An on-screen four-function calculator is provided. The traps are usually
careless arithmetic (sign errors, decimal placement) rather than missing
mathematical knowledge. Time pressure is brutal — averages out to roughly
67 seconds per question.
