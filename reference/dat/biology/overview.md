# DAT Biology — Section Overview

This stub exists so the corpus loader has content to chunk during Phase 0
verification. Replace with real reference material as authoring proceeds.

## Topic scope (per ADA blueprint, 40 questions, 90-minute section)

- **Cell & Molecular Biology** — cell structure, membrane transport,
  signaling, the cell cycle, enzymes, photosynthesis, cellular respiration.
- **Diversity of Life** — taxonomy and classification across the major
  kingdoms; defining features of bacteria, archaea, fungi, plants, animals.
- **Vertebrate Anatomy & Physiology** — circulatory, respiratory, digestive,
  excretory, nervous, endocrine, reproductive, integumentary, muscular,
  skeletal systems.
- **Developmental Biology** — gametogenesis, fertilization, embryonic
  development, germ-layer derivatives.
- **Genetics** — Mendelian inheritance, chromosomal genetics, molecular
  genetics, population genetics, gene regulation.
- **Evolution, Ecology & Behavior** — natural selection, speciation,
  population ecology, community interactions, animal behavior.

## Question pattern notes

Stems are short to moderate length. Most questions test recall of a
mechanism, structure, or concept, with a sprinkling of multi-step reasoning
items that stack two facts. Trap distractors typically swap the direction
of a process (e.g. depolarization vs. repolarization) or invert a regulatory
relationship.
