# DAT Organic Chemistry — Section Overview

Stub for Phase 0 corpus-loader verification. Replace with real reference
material as authoring proceeds.

## Topic scope (per ADA blueprint, 30 questions inside the 90-minute SNS)

- **Mechanisms** — energetics, structure effects, arrow pushing, common
  intermediates (carbocations, carbanions, free radicals).
- **Chemical & Physical Properties** — boiling and melting points,
  solubility, spectroscopy (IR, NMR, MS basics).
- **Stereochemistry** — chirality, R/S, E/Z, optical activity, meso compounds,
  enantiomers vs. diastereomers.
- **Nomenclature** — IUPAC for major functional groups.
- **Reactions of Major Functional Groups** — addition, substitution
  (SN1/SN2), elimination (E1/E2), oxidation, reduction, condensation;
  alkenes, alkynes, alcohols, ethers, carbonyls, carboxylic acid derivatives,
  amines, aromatics.
- **Acid-Base Chemistry** — pKa ranking, conjugate acid/base reasoning,
  Bronsted-Lowry vs. Lewis acidity.
- **Aromatics & Bonding** — Hückel's rule, resonance, electrophilic aromatic
  substitution.

## Question pattern notes

The hardest section for many test-takers. Items often present a starting
material plus reagent and ask for the product, or vice versa. Distractors
exploit ortho/para vs. meta directing effects, stereochemistry inversion,
and regioselectivity (Markovnikov vs. anti-Markovnikov).
