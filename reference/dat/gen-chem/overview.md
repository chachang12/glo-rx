# DAT General Chemistry — Section Overview

Stub for Phase 0 corpus-loader verification. Replace with real reference

## Topic scope (per ADA blueprint, 30 questions inside the 90-minute SNS)

- **Stoichiometry & General Concepts** — moles, percent composition,
  empirical and molecular formulas, balancing equations, limiting reagent.
- **Gases** — ideal gas law, partial pressures, kinetic molecular theory.
- **Liquids & Solids** — intermolecular forces, phase diagrams, vapor
  pressure.
- **Solutions** — molarity, molality, colligative properties, solubility.
- **Acids & Bases** — pH, pKa, buffers, titration curves.
- **Chemical Equilibria** — Keq, Le Chatelier, solubility product.
- **Thermodynamics & Thermochemistry** — enthalpy, entropy, Gibbs free energy.
- **Chemical Kinetics** — rate laws, reaction order, activation energy.
- **Redox Reactions** — half-reactions, electrochemistry, standard potentials.
- **Atomic & Molecular Structure** — quantum numbers, periodic trends,
  Lewis structures, VSEPR, hybridization.
- **Periodic Properties** — periodicity of atomic radius, ionization energy,
  electronegativity, electron affinity.
- **Nuclear Reactions** — alpha/beta/gamma decay, half-life.
- **Laboratory** — common techniques, safety, basic instrumentation.

## Question pattern notes

Calculation-heavy compared to Biology. Most stems can be solved without a
calculator using rounded estimates; numerical answer choices are usually
spread far enough to allow approximation.
